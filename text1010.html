NAME and friends both enjoy Rubik's cubes. They want to create a fun rubiks cube statue, model, effigy giant version or a small version with an interesting material.

The conditions are:

  • It has to be a cube
  • It does not have to work
  • It costs $50\$50$50 to enter

They know the competition will be marked on:

  • How unique the idea is
  • How big the cube is

Brainstorm some different statue ideas or the competition

Select the group's favourite idea.

The blueprints of the statue will show roughly what the cube with be made from, and how it will look when put together, both inside and outside.

Sketch out the blueprints.
